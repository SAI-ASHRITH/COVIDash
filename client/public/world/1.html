No proof from US on claims about coronavirus originating from Chinese lab: WHO

World Health Organization emergencies director Michael Ryan on Monday said that the US had provided no evidence to support "speculative" claims by US President Donald Trump that coronavirus originated in a lab in China's Wuhan. "We have not received any data or specific evidence from the US...so from our perspective this remains speculative," he said.

Nearly 4,800 Indians are COVID-19 positive in Singapore: Envoy

Indian High Commissioner to Singapore, Jawed Ashraf, has said nearly 4,800 Indian nationals have tested positive for coronavirus in Singapore till the end of April. Over 90% of them are living in dormitories for foreign workers, he added. Further, he said, over 3,500 Indian nationals, including students, have registered to return home or have sought assistance for accommodation and food.
