Coronavirus has shown us limitation of existing international system: PM Modi

While addressing Non-Aligned Movement Summit through video conferencing, PM Narendra Modi said, "COVID-19 has shown us the limitation of existing international system." He added, "In the post COVID-19 world, we need a new template of globalisation based on fairness, equality and humanity." "We need international institutions that are more representative of today's world," he further said.

India regarded as pharmacy of world, sent medical supplies to 123 countries: PM

PM Narendra Modi on Monday spoke at Non-Aligned Movement (NAM) Summit via video-conferencing where he said, "India is regarded as the pharmacy of the world, especially for affordable medicines." He added, "Despite our own needs, we have ensured medical supplies to 123 partner countries, including 59 NAM members." The NAM comprises 120 developing nations from Asia, Africa and Latin America.
