13 Sashastra Seema Bal jawans test positive for coronavirus

As many as 13 personnel of the border guarding force Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) have tested positive for coronavirus till now, said an official on Monday. All infected personnel belong to the 25th battalion of the force. Twenty-nine SSB jawans, who were quarantined after one jawan complained of fever and cough last week, were all tested for the virus.

Coronavirus cases in Gujarat rise to 5,804; death toll rises to 319

Gujarat on Monday recorded 376 new coronavirus cases, taking the total number of cases there to 5,804, said a state health department bulletin. Gujarat's coronavirus death toll rose to 319, after 29 people died in the state in a single day. Ahmedabad reported the highest number of new cases, with 259 new coronavirus cases reported there on Monday.

Will seal areas where social distancing norms are violated: CM Kejriwal

Referring to social distancing norms being violated in certain areas of Delhi today, Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal warned that the government will seal the area and revoke relaxations where rules are flouted. "Shop owners will have to take the responsibility, if the norms of social distancing are violated outside a shop then the shop will be shut," he added.
